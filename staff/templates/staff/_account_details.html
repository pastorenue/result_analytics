FAQs

How do I renew my payments?

You can subscribe to a plan [Monthly, Semesterly, or Sessionally] and we will notify you once your subscription is about to expire or you can pay everytime your payment expires

How often do I get to view my results?

Your results are always live the moment your school uploads them. If you are operating a custom account, you get to see your analysis as soon as you upload your results.

Can I collaborate with my lecturers?

One of the essence of acadlytics is for collaboration. It is to ensure an effective study admosphere for students among their peers and lecturers even with lecturers that are not of your institution.
